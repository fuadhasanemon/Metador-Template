About Us

WHY US

Pearl Harbour, an aristocratic air-conditioned community Centre with world class interior decoration, is a prestigious venue for your communal and corporate gathering.

Enjoy the unique architectural beauty, crafted by a renowned Bangladeshi architect and the warm hospitality provides by our professional team that makes your event grand and memorable.

MD. SHAH ALAM

Chairman of Matador Group

Md Shah Alam is the founder Chairman of Matador Group. The group has diversified under his keen leadership since 1982. Professionally he started his career as a renowned advocate of Bangladesh Supreme Court. After 25 years of legal practice, he decided to start a family company through manufacturing of plastic combs. After that, new business units were set up for producing, selling and marketing of toothbrushes, stationery, building materials, food etc.

He graduated from the Department of Law, Dhaka University and became FIRST CLASS FIRST in his L.L.B degree.
